***This file contains information about the raw data sources used for this project

*2005_Street_Tree_Census.csv: Census of trees in New York City in 2005
	https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Environment/2005-Street-Tree-Census/29bw-z7pj
*ACS_raw.csv: 2007-2011 and 2014-2018 American Community Survey data
*ARCHIVED - Parks Properties: Shapefile of parks owned by the City of New York
	https://data.cityofnewyork.us/City-Government/ARCHIVED-Parks-Properties/k2ya-ucmv
*arrests.csv: geocoded arrest data
	https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Public-Safety/NYPD-Arrests-Data-Historic-/8h9b-rp9u
*Boundaries: Census tract boundaries Shapefile
*cbd.csv: Obtained from Daniel Hartley @ Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago
*graduation_rates.xlsx: High school graduation rates by public school district
	https://infohub.nyced.org/reports/academics/graduation-results
*housing_violations.csv: Housing maintenance violations for rental properties
	https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Housing-Development/Housing-Maintenance-Code-Violations/wvxf-dwi5
*ltdb_dp.xlsx: Longitudinal Tract Database-Differential Privacy for 2000 Census
	https://s4.ad.brown.edu/projects/diversity/researcher/LTDB.htm
*NYC_BICYCLE_NETWORK_18D_20200214_p: Shapefile of bike lanes in New York City
	https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Transportation/Bicycle-Routes/7vsa-caz7
	archived_nyc_bike_routes_2010.zip
*nynta_21b: New York City neighborhood tabulation area shapefile
	https://www1.nyc.gov/site/planning/data-maps/open-data/dwn-nynta.page
*permit_issuances: construction permits issued by the department of buildings for the 2009-2011 period
	https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Housing-Development/DOB-Permit-Issuance/ipu4-2q9a/data
*pluto: Extensive land use and geographic data at the tax lot level
	https://www1.nyc.gov/site/planning/data-maps/open-data/bytes-archive.page?sorts[year]=0
*station_entrances.csv: Location of subway station entrances
	http://web.mta.info/developers/sbwy_entrance.html
*WAC: Longitudinal Employer-Household Dynamics Origin Destination Statistics
